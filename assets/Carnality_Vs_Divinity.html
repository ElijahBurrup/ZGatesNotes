The Core

The Shift Is Not What You Think It Is

It is not a move of God. It is a move in your understanding.

When you hear the word "shift" in church, your mind goes to revival. The spirit falls. People are healed. The room shakes. But the shift God is bringing right now is different. It is not an event. It is an awareness.

The shift is in how you perceive. How you see. How you understand who you are and what you carry. Once you get a certain level of knowledge, your life begins to change. You stop perishing. You stop going through things you don't need to go through because you finally have an understanding.

Hosea 4:6

"My people perish for lack of knowledge."

This Changes Everything

If you want grace and you want peace, you need the knowledge of God. You cannot remain ignorant and ask for more peace. Grace and peace are multiplied to you in the knowledge of God. The more knowledge, the more understanding you have of the Christ within — the stronger you walk, the more you take.

This teaching is going to anchor us in our true identity as believers in Christ.
Pastor Tasha

God Always Follows the Same Blueprint

He calls a man. Consecrates. Fills. Sends. Takes a region.

Every time God wants to do something in the earth, He follows the same pattern. Over and over in scripture — the same sequence. He doesn't need a mass of people. He needs a few who will sanctify themselves.

That individual has to be consecrated to the Lord. Nothing can move them. Money can't move them. Titles can't move them. Positions can't move them. They are not tied to this world. They know they are here for a reason, and that reason is His mandate.

God finds that person, sanctifies them, prunes them, deals with deep heart issues, mindsets, lies they believed, identity issues — and brings them into alignment with who He says they are. Then He puts His Spirit upon and in that person. Then He sends them to a people.

The Commission

God speaks to one. The one speaks to a group. The group sanctifies themselves. They take the land.

Put away the stuff that defiles you. Anything that's not like Him, put it away. Anything that would grieve Him, walk away from it. Die to it. Because God wants to do something great in our midst.

This is what we see with Joshua. Moses dies. God calls Joshua, charges him, commissions him. Then Joshua goes to the people and tells them: Sanctify yourselves. Because God wants to do wonders.

We see the same pattern with every person God raised up. He doesn't need a mass — He needs a few who will consecrate themselves and carry His glory.

Joshua

Apprentice to Leader

Had to shift his mind from serving Moses to leading millions. God charged him: "Be strong and courageous." Then sent him to the people to take the land.

Gideon

300 Against 120,000

Hiding in a winepress. God called him "man of valor." Reduced his army from thousands to 300. The Midianites fell like sand — it is not by might, not by power, but by My Spirit.

Moses

"You Will Be As God"

Take your shoes off — don't bring where you've been into this holy place. God shifted his identity: "You will be as God to Pharaoh. When you speak, I move."

Samson

The Spirit of Might

Ropes, shackles, every kind of bondage — broken like nothing. God giving us a picture: nothing should be able to hold you. The spirit of might breaks it off.

Elijah

Life Resurrecting Life

Called down fire. Laid on the dead child of the widow — the life of God on him resurrected that child. The life upon us is supposed to resurrect life in those who are dying.

Peter & Paul

Shadow & Handkerchief

Peter's shadow healed people as he walked by. Paul couldn't reach everyone, so handkerchiefs that touched his body healed the sick. It has always been God's will to work through us.

We have to adjust to greatness. Mediocrity is so normal. Paycheck to paycheck is so normal. You have to adjust to victory. You have to adjust to dominion.
Pastor Tasha
The Wineskin

The Old System Cannot Contain What God Is Pouring

You cannot put new wine into old wineskins.
Mark 2:22

"No one puts new wine into old wineskins, or else the new wine will burst the wineskins and the wine is spilled, and the wineskins are ruined. But new wine must be put into new wineskins."

Old wineskins are dry and rigid. New wine ferments, bubbles, needs room to expand. Jesus is using this to describe people who are stuck — stuck in the same prayer, the same worship, the same mentality, the same level of revelation. And God is trying to pour something new, and the old container cannot hold it.

Old Wineskin

Salvation-focused Christianity

All have sinned — so we preach blood, repentance, grace, mercy. Get saved, do Bible study, get plugged into church. But we have not taught how to walk in the glory that we lost.

New Wineskin

Walking in the glory

Christ in you is the hope of restoring that glory. Not just stopping sin — but walking in dominion, execution, and the divine capacity that comes from being one with Him.

Romans 3:23

"For all have sinned and fall short of the glory of God."

The Missing Half

We preached the solution to sin. We never preached the restoration of glory.

Western Christianity captured the sin problem but missed the glory problem. All have sinned and fall short of the glory. There's the sin issue — then there's the dominion issue. The glory issue. The standard-of-living issue. We've been treating half the diagnosis.

If you are demanding things go your way, operating according to how you've always done it, you will look at the end of your life and all you will have is regrets. You live one time. You don't get to do 25 over. The level of impact at the end of your life depends on whether you were able to shed the old, take on the new — and move with God.

Revival is yesterday's move. Revival days are over. It's the glory days now. The individuals have to carry the presence. I have to carry God.
Pastor Tasha
The Revelation

Christ In You — The Hope of Glory

Not the seated Christ. The Christ within.
Colossians 1:26–27

"The mystery which has been hidden from ages and from generations, but now has been revealed to His saints — Christ in you, the hope of glory."

We think "the hope of glory" means making it to heaven. It does not. All have sinned and fallen short of a standard of living, a standard of execution, a kind of walk, a level of dominion. But Christ in you is the hope of you restoring that place of dominion.

That is why Paul can turn around and say "I can do all things through Christ." It is Christ in you. Not the seated Christ you pray up to. The seated Christ is saying: you are filled with my Spirit. Rise up and walk. Do it. Speak to it. If you speak to it, my Spirit within you is addressing it.

"Where my strength ends, His begins."
Christ-Consciousness
Shifting from self — my failures, my insecurities, my weakness — to the Christ within. There is another power on the inside. Another strength. Where your capacity ends, His begins. He completes you.

The Original Design

You Were Never Meant to Operate As a Mere Human

God gave Adam instruction based on divine capacity, not human limits.

When God created Adam before the fall, there was a oneness with God. Adam's spirit was one with God's Spirit. So when God gave instruction, He did not give it based on human capacity. He gave it based on the fact that you are linked with Me.

Subdue the earth. Not your backyard — the earth. Name every creature. That's taxonomy — calling each thing according to its essence. This is not something one person does unless you have divine capacity. Unless you are linked to a source that empowers you to do more than a human can do, to see more than a human can see, to take more than a human can take.

What We Lost

Falling from the glory means losing the capacity that came from being linked with God.

Now you operate as mere men. You can't handle much. You can't take much. "I can't deal with this." "It's too much." "I'm overwhelmed." That's a human being operating without divine capacity.

But when you were linked with Him — there was capacity. God's heart is not just for you to stop sinning. You can't stop sinning unless you have divine capacity over sin. That's why you fall, repent, fall, repent, fall, repent — over and over. You have no capacity over sin.

The Real Question

God never intended to work apart from us. From the very beginning, His design was oneness. Every miracle in scripture — Moses, Gideon, Samson, Elijah, Peter, Paul — was just God giving us a glimpse of what becomes possible when a person is linked to the divine. And none of them even had what we have. In the Old Testament, the Spirit came upon. Today, the Spirit lives within.

There has to be a house in Tulsa that carries the glory.

There are over 4,000 churches in Tulsa County. There has to be a house that carries the glory. Not about numbers. Not about fame. Not about preaching self-help. A house that is 100% focused on the kingdom of God and the glory of God. A house willing to sacrifice until we see the manifestation of the hand of God.

It is not by might. It is not by power. It is by My Spirit, says the Lord.
Zechariah 4:6

God spoke to Tasha. Tasha is speaking to you. God wants to take this city. And the enemy wants you to see yourself as a grasshopper — too little, not enough, too weak, too divided. Die to that grasshopper mentality. It is not by might, not by connections, not by money. It is by the very Spirit of the Lord.

The Commitment

Tuesday. All day. Every week.

We have committed to tithe one day a week. Tuesday — fasting the entire day, from the moment your eyes open to the moment your eyes close. Then come together at 7:30 for prayer.

This is not fasting for your issues. This is not self-focused prayer. This is focused on the Christ within. So that God can sanctify us, consecrate us, and the revelation of what is being taught can come alive on the inside.

The City on a Hill

Before Jesus talks about a city set on a hill, He talks about a person. "You are the light of the world." The city is always tied to a person. If God wants a city, He first has to find a person. That person becomes the light. The Spirit on that one person ignites many others. And with the others, He takes the city.

This Has To Become Reality

It's not going to work as head knowledge. It's not going to work with you just saying "Amen, good word Pastor." It has to become reality. You have to become it. And that requires spending time on your face before the Lord. Not fasting for your problems — focused on the Christ within.

Partakers of the Divine Nature

The shift is not an event. It is a revelation. When it moves from your head to your heart — when you stop seeing yourself and start seeing the Christ within — it's over. For Tulsa. For the enemy. For every limitation you thought defined you.
